‘Please do,’ Alice said very politely.

The White Queen laughed with delight, and stroked Alice’s cheek. Then she began:

‘“First, the fish must be caught.”

That is easy: a baby, I think, could have caught it. “Next, the fish must be bought.” That is easy: a penny, I think, would have bought it.

“Now cook me the fish!”

That is easy, and will not take more than a minute. “Let it lie in a dish!” That is easy, because it already is in it.

“Bring it here! Let me sup!”

It is easy to set such a dish on the table. “Take the dish-cover up!” Ah, that is so hard that I fear I’m unable!
